### What we do

**Core Mission**
Practi.City serves as a collaborative, open-contribution community, knowledge hub, and code repository dedicated to promoting and advancing experiential learning as the premier method for acquiring professional skills. 

**Key Goals**
1. **Curates an Open-Source Knowledge Base:** It catalogs and deeply describes open-source experiential learning simulation tools (such as JaamSim and NetLogo) and experiential learning methodologies (such as Practice Firms and advanced role-play).
2. **Shares Community Stories:** It blogs about and highlights the organizations, initiatives, and people that actively use these tools and methodologies in the real world.
3. **Develops Open-Source Code:** It publishes, shares, and maintains code that extends existing simulation software (like JaamSim or DWSIM) and builds interfaces that connect these various solutions together.
4. **Integrates AI:** It promotes new AI practices and explores how artificial intelligence expands the possibilities of experiential learning and simulation.
5. **Establishes a Universal Manifesto:** It provides a foundational set of rules and principles that allow users to seamlessly combine their respective experiential learning paths and share them collaboratively. 

**Structure & Governance**
*   **Licensing:** All resources and code are freely available for both commercial and non-commercial use, with the sole requirement of proper attribution to **PRACTI.CITY**.
*   **Hosting:** The platform and all its publications are hosted efficiently and transparently on GitHub Pages.
*   **Management:** A French non-profit organization (Association Loi 1901) based in Lyon, France, hosts and manages the community and platform.



### The Legal Stuff

Practi.City is a NON PROFIT ORGANISATION registered in Lyon, France under the name Assocation EASE "Practi.City" since may 2026 placed under the '1901 law' (loi de 1901, yes this is a long time ago)
